Site Name

Name: TrailVibes

This name captures the outdoor and energetic vibe of a site dedicated to trail reviews and hiking tips.

Site Purpose

TrailVibes is a go-to hub for nature lovers, offering curated hiking trail reviews, gear tips, and a form to submit personal trail experiences.

Scenarios

  • What are the best beginner-friendly hikes in my area?
  • How can I share my trail experience with the community?

Color Scheme

  • Forest Green (#2d6a4f): Used for headings and primary navigation.
  • Earth Beige (#fefae0): Used for background and content areas.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat (used for all titles and nav items)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (used for paragraphs and general content)
